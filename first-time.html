What to Expect Your First Time

Your first session with Approachable Fit is all about getting to know you and setting the foundation for your fitness journey. Here’s what you can expect:

  • 1. Warm Welcome

    You’ll meet your trainer, Mark, who will create a comfortable and supportive environment. We’ll discuss your goals, preferences, and any concerns to ensure a personalized experience.

  • 2. Goal Setting

    Together, we’ll outline your short- and long-term fitness goals. Whether it’s building strength, improving endurance, something highly specific, or enhancing overall health, we’ll create a clear plan to get you there.

  • 3. Fitness Assessment

    We’ll conduct a gentle assessment of your current fitness level, including basic movements and flexibility. This helps us tailor a program that’s right for you, whether you’re a beginner or experienced.

  • 4. Next Steps

    We’ll wrap up with a discussion of your personalized plan, including session frequency, schedule, and how we’ll track progress. You’ll leave feeling excited and ready to start!

No need to prepare—just bring comfortable workout clothes, a water bottle, and an open mind. We’ll take care of the rest!
